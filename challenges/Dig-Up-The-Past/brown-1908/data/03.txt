June 10th
Passed Dirty Woman Ranch about 8 o'clock. Stopped and wrote letters and talked with Mrs. Low, a young matron of 18 daughter of Mr. Cunningham of Cow-Rock creek. Roads were good all day over the divide + down U-All Creek. Camped about five miles above the mouth of the Creek.


Government  insurance against hail costs 20-30+40 cts. per acre against total loss in which case farmer receives $2, $6, or $8 per acre. In case only partially injured a  government adjustor adjuges per centage.

Wheat is sold through government inspection
	Inspectors grade wheat at different points and farmer can call for inspectors grade if dissatisfied with mill grade. 
	All milling + elevator men must be licensed + bonded. Commission merchants charge 1 ct per bu. on what + 1/2 ct on oats.
	Oats seldom used for milling on account of stooling which usually catches some frost.

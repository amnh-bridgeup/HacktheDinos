
Deller Mastodon
Found by Mr. Walker Deller on fathers farm 3 miles north of Ashley, Steuben Co. Indiana, 1908
Struck skull while digging drainage ditch six feet below surface. skull was upside down with right tusk in place 9 feet long and point turned out according to Mr. Deller. Mr. Ed Ferrier of Flint, Ind. secured missing tooth

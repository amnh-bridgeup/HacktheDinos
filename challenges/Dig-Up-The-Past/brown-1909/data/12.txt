
Cretaceous Dinosaurs in Arkansas
In 1903 Prof. Branner of Leland Stanford University discovered Dinosaur bones in the lower Cret. beds of Pike Co. Arkansas near Murfresboro. They were described by him as being very large and quite numerous. The locality is between two and four miles South east of the town on the Little Missouri River near the mouth of  _______ Creek not far from a fill composed of eruption rock. near by is a bed of gypsum from 4 to 6 feet thick.

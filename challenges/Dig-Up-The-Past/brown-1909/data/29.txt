
Judith River Trachodons
Part of skeleton in sandstone good bones uncrushed. Pelvis and part of vertebrae uncovered and then covered up. Flatwillow Creek N. of bend in Musselshell and about 10 miles from ranch. Found in 1903.

Parts of 2 skeletons 40 caudals somewhat crushed, in clay close to wad near J.L. Wagner's house N. side of Red Deer River and pelvis and six feet of caudals in pasture of S. Skuce south side of Red Deer. 1/2 mile west of house in main bluff near 2nd coulee from right, house, hidden by large butte from specimen. Small skeleton not identified but ceratopsian teeth with bones just below Trachodon in white sandstone.

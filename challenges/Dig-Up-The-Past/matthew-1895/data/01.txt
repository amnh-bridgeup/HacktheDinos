
Notes of 
Egypt Trip 1895 
 
Worked first at mule-stable 
on 3rd level (new mule-road). 
Found here a number of 
scattered and badly broken 
bones, including jaw bones.  Remains 
are few and scattered, and not 
worth looking into further 
Worked next on 3rd level 
at the corner of second slope 
to eastward.  This is well 
towards E. end of mine.  Nearly 
a day's work put in here, and 
not a single distinctly 
recognizable bone found, tho' 
a number of teeth.  The 6-inch 
seam is very thin and poor 
here.

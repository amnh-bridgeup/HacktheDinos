343. Broken tooth. See below.

	The locality examined here is on the  
NE side of the Rio Descado.  Where the  
road from the RR Station of Pico Truncado  
descends from the Pampa to the river valley,  
just west of the road. 
	The section is : 
                     Pataganiano  
            Parallel uncomformity       
   125' tuffs and tuffaceous clays, white or  
pale, bedded & with several layers tosquilla- 
like, less than 1 m. thick. 
      Apparent perfect conformity 
	100'+ "Argiles Fissilaires" typical,  
yellow, red & whitish, in part very hard  
? with small limonite nodules. 
                 Base not exposed

bones, but very few #343, the only

specimen found, comes froma bout 90' up in
